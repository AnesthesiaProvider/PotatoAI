Common Potato Diseases

Our model can detect these common potato diseases with high accuracy.

Early Blight

Caused by Alternaria solani, appears as small, dark lesions with concentric rings.

Treatment: Fungicides

Late Blight

Caused by Phytophthora infestans, appears as water-soaked lesions that turn brown.

Treatment: Remove infected plants

Blackleg

Caused by bacteria, appears as black, rotting stems at the base of the plant.

Treatment: Remove infected plants

Healthy

Healthy potato leaves are vibrant green with no spots, lesions, or discoloration.

No treatment needed

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our potato disease detection system.

Our model achieves an accuracy of over 95% in laboratory conditions. In real-world scenarios, accuracy may vary depending on image quality and environmental factors. We continuously improve the model with new data.

Currently, our system can detect Early Blight, Late Blight, and Blackleg diseases in potato plants. We're working to expand detection capabilities to include more diseases in future updates.

For best results, upload clear, well-lit photos of individual potato leaves against a neutral background. The leaf should fill most of the frame. Avoid blurry images or those with multiple leaves overlapping.

Images are processed in real-time and are not stored permanently unless you opt-in to help improve our model. All data is handled according to our strict privacy policy.
